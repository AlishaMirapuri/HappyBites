Best Happy Hour Deals in NYC (2024)

Updated March 2024 · By the FoodieGuide editorial team

We scoured every neighbourhood from Tribeca to Astoria to find the city's best happy hour deals — here are our top picks.

1. The Spotted Dog — Tribeca

Head here weekdays from 5 to 7pm for $5 draft beers, $6 house wines, and half-price sliders. Dine-in only; limit 2 rounds per table.

2. Masa's Cantina — East Village

Monday through Thursday, 4–6pm happy hour at the bar.

Deals include: margaritas for $7, nachos $6, tacos 2-for-1.

Bar seating only. No reservations accepted.

3. The Pearl Oyster Bar — West Village

Oyster happy hour daily after 5! Half-dozen oysters for $12 (reg. $24), plus $8 white wines. No reservations needed.

4. Maple Street Cafe — Park Slope

Weekend brunch specials every Saturday and Sunday, 10am–2pm. Bottomless mimosas $22, brunch combo plates from $14–$18.

5. Noodle Bar Happy Hour

Every day from 3 to 6 PM: ramen bowls $10 (reg. $16), gyoza 6-piece $5.

6. Sullivan Street Bakery Café

Deal: Lunch special — weekdays only —  Noon to 2pm.
Was $15 now $11. Includes sandwich, cookie, & coffee.
* Not available on holidays.

7. Le Jardin — Midtown

Early bird prix fixe dinner every Tuesday through Thursday, 5:00pm to 6:30pm. Three-course menu: $45 per person. Includes soup or salad, entrée, and dessert. Reservations required.
