Best Happy Hour & Food Deals in San Francisco (2024)

Updated March 2024 · BayGuide editorial team

From the Mission to the Marina, SF's food scene is packed with deals — if you know where to look. Here are our favourite value spots right now.

1. Tacos El Gordo — Mission District

Daily lunch special from 11am to 3pm: any three tacos for $10 (regular price $4.50 each). Best al pastor in the city, full stop. Cash preferred.

2. Blue Bottle Coffee — SoMa

Happy hour weekdays 2–4pm: drip coffee and cold brew for $2 (normally $4–$5). Pair with a croissant for $4. Limited to the Mint Plaza flagship location.

3. Mighty Burger — Valencia Corridor

Monday through Friday, 4pm to 6pm: double smashburger combo (burger + fries + drink) for $14. Weekday only; was $22 at dinner price. No coupon needed.

4. The Interval at Long Now — Fort Mason

Bar happy hour Tuesday through Saturday 5–7pm. Draft beers $5, house wines $7, and a rotating selection of small plates at half price. Members get an extra $2 off.

5. Flores — Upper Fillmore

Margarita Mondays: build-your-own margaritas from $8 all day on Mondays. Their guacamole and chips comes down to $6 (was $12). Dine-in only, no reservations.

6. Swan Oyster Depot — Nob Hill

Not technically happy hour, but their half-dozen oysters run $15–$18 depending on variety — significantly below comparable spots. Opens at 10:30am, closes when they sell out (usually around 2pm). Cash only. Get there early.

7. Che Fico Alimentari — Divisadero

Weekday lunch special noon to 2:30pm: any pasta dish plus a small salad for $18 (dinner equivalent is $28–$32). Reservations not accepted for lunch; first come, first served.
